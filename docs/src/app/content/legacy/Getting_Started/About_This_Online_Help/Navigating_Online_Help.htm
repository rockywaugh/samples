Navigating Online Help

This Online Help system provides a Table of Contents (TOC) with topic links, and a Search function.

Click the Book icon ()  or the topic name to see an overview of the topic and to expand the topic. Then select from the list of sub-topics.

In addition, many topics/pages contain links within the text to other topics. These links are shown in blue text and are underlined. Example links are shown below.

In addition to using the Table of Contents to navigate to topics, you can also use the Search function.

  1. Click the Search tab at the top of the Online Help, as shown below.

  2. Enter a search term or phrase in the Search field, and press [Enter]. RIO displays a results list with all pages containing your search term.

  3. Click a selection in the results list to view the page.
