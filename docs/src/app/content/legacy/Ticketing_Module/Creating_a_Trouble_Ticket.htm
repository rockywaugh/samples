Creating a Trouble Ticket

The Trouble Ticket Surveys provide forms for creating a ticket in Remedy. These forms are customized by Survey Group. Surveys are for TOC and Care Bridge teams to escalate issues to the NOC. Submitting a survey creates a ticket and begins a work flow.

Do not submit a survey unless you require a ticket to be escalated.

Although you can submit an Eventrak ticket from RIO, you cannot update or close an Eventrak ticket from RIO.

 To create a Trouble ticket:

From the Menu, select Fault Management>Trouble Ticket Survey Groups. RIO displays the Survey Groups tab.

Select your applicable Survey Group from the left column, by clicking the hyper-linked group name. RIO displays a list of Surveys for the selected team, along with a description of each. A sample for the Care Survey Group is shown below.

Click the Create a Ticket link for the appropriate Survey. RIO displays the Create Ticket form.

Complete the required fields on the form. The fields vary depending on the Survey, so be sure to use the scroll bar, if applicable, to view all fields.

Click Save. RIO submits the ticket to a queue for processing. After the ticket is successfully submitted to Remedy, RIO displays a notification.

Click the Notifications icon () in the toolbar.

In some rare instances, you may receive a time-out message indicating that RIO did not receive a response from Remedy that the ticket was created. In these cases, check Remedy to verify if the ticket was actually created before resubmitting another ticket from RIO. This helps avoid duplicate ticket entries.
