Family Tree Tab

The Family Tree in RIO allows you to see relationships among entities.  This tab (available from the Site down to the Account level) displays the current entity, plus the Parents, Siblings, and Children of the selected entity.

In the Network Diagnostics module, the Family Tree tab in a View (e.g. Hub, Node, CMTS, Account, etc.) shows the relationship of devices as listed in the following table.

To view the Family Tree, select the Family Tree tab in an entity View or select Family Tree from the Service Path drop-down menu. A sample Node Family Tree is shown below.

Once you are in the Family Tree view, clicking on entity links within the tree takes you to the Family Tree for that specific entity, so you can start from the top Site level and drill down through the Family Tree to other levels.

In the Cable Modem and Set-top Box views, the Family tree also displays online status of the device's siblings (Cable Modem, STB, or MTA). A sample is shown below.

A gray icon indicates the status is unknown.

Health icons are available for Siblings and Children device entities at the Account level (e.g. cable modems, set-top boxes, etc.).
