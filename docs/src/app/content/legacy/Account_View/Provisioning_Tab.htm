Provisioning Tab

The Provisioning tab in the Account View displays more detailed device provisioning information. A partial sample is shown below.

The Provisioning tab contains the following panels.

Panel

Displays

Subscriber Details

Account Number, Name and Address, TN and Account Status.

Service Details

Domain, max number of users allowed on the account, status of service, registration, and reason for last registration

User Accounts

Master and any sub-accounts, including status and linked e-mail address.

The Master Account is indicated by the star icon ( ).

See Promoting a Sub-Account to Master.

Devices

Device ID, type, services, quarantine status and device status.

Email Info

Email address and mailbox information for the account.

Account Services

Service codes on the account identify customer type, ISP, service type, etc.

Digital Home Phone

Digital Phone number and features.

Phone Line Details

Line features and properties plus service information for each line on the account.
