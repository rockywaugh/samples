Device Watch Tab

The Device Watch tab in the Account view displays the Device Watches that are currently configured for devices on the Account.

To view only watches you have set up for the device, click My Watch.

Click on a hyper-linked Account Number or MAC Address to jump to the Account or Device Summary page.

To stop the watch, click Running in the Status column, and then click Stop Watch.

You can stop only watches that you have configured.

To view additional details, click More+ in the Watch Results column.

For additional details, see the Device Watch tab in the Cable Modem view.
