Account View Overview

The Account View is a tabbed view that contains information related to a specific subscriber account. You access this view by clicking any Account Number hyperlink on other pages (e.g. Family Tree, Subscriber Listings, etc.), or you can search for a specific account using the Search Function in the Toolbar.

New account or service information may not be available in RIO for up to 36 hours. In these instances, you can access the service information by other means (for example, via NYROC).

RIO displays information for both residential billing Accounts and Salesforce Accounts (Commercial Account Tab).

The residential Account view includes the following tabs:

Summary (default landing page)

Signal Levels

Provisioning

Entitlements Tab

IVR Calls

Device Watch

Work Orders

Family Tree

Entity History
