Promoting a Sub-Account to Master

If the customer account contains one or more sub-account(s), RIO provides the capability to promote a sub-account to be the master account. The Master Account is indicated by the star () icon.

To promote a sub-account:

In the Account View, select the Provisioning tab.

In the User Accounts panel, click the pencil icon () next to the sub-account you want to promote. RIO displays a confirmation message.

Click Promote. RIO updates the Sub Type to Master and changes the previous Master account to Sub Account.
