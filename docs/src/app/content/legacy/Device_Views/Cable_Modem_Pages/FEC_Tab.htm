FEC Tab

The Forward Error Correction (FEC) tab provides information on the amount of Cable Modem data in error that was corrected and uncorrected during the FEC process. This tab also displays FEC reported by the CMTS for the selected Cable Modem.

Data is available to view after the second poll is taken, which occurs approximately 5 seconds after selecting the tab for viewing.

RIO also provides a pop-out window for viewing more detailed information. Click the Pop-out Window () to view. A sample chart is shown below.

The charts are refreshed approximately every 10 seconds.

Use the scroll bar to view additional Codeword graphs. Graphs available include: Total, Unerrored, Corrected, and Uncorrectable Codewords.

Hover over points on the graphs to view additional pop-up information.

Select MHz options to view/clear by clicking on the corresponding color in the MHz legend.

Click the up/down arrows () to expand/collapse the graphs.
