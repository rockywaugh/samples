Simple Summary Tab

The Simple Summary tab in the Cable Modem view is the default landing page for select user groups.

This page is also available to other users and is listed in the Service Path drop-down list. You can also add it to your view by changing your View Preferences.

This tab displays the following panels:

Panel

Displays . . .

Realtime

CM Init state, ping status, uptime, and whether the modem is in quarantine. Items in this panel auto-refresh every 5 seconds. Click the Pop-out Window icon to view in a separate window.

Health

Health status bar of the modem for the last for the last 24 hours, 3 days, and 7 days.

This tab displays the following Action buttons:

Action

Allows you to . . .

Quarantine/Unquarantine

Quarantine (disable) a modem or enable a modem that has been quarantined.

Note: The Quarantine action is available only to users with special permissions for this feature

Reset

Reboot the modem or MTA.
