Child Devices

The Component Status panel displays any devices that have a child relationship to the cable modem. For example, if the TWC customer subscribes to Home Phone, RIO displays the MTA information.

In addition, the ADSG and Hydra STBs are listed as Child Devices to the ADSG and Hydra cable modems respectively.
