IP STB Summary Tab

The Summary tab in the IP Set-top Box view provides information on the customer’s video services. Depending on the platform and technology of the box, the Summary tab data varies as RIO displays the data as available from the device’s response.

If the set-top box (STB) is an IP STB connected to a Hydra-class STB, RIO displays the IP STB tab. A sample is shown below.

The IP STB tab includes the following panels:

Panel

Displays . . .

Status

Online status, vendor, IP address(es), model and MAC address, plus CPU usage and Memory.

MoCA Summary

Multi-media over Coax Alliance (MoCA) summary information for high definition video content; including packets sent and received, bit rate, and operating frequency.

UPNP

Universal Plug and Play (UPNP) status.

Browser

The Window status. This information is pulled from DSB, ACS, Device Management Cert2Device, and the PI Gateway.

SPP

Set-top box type, version, model and address information, and customer service information for credit and PPV limits.

Boot Log

Uptime and date/time and reason the device was rebooted. This information is pulled from DSB, ACS, Device Management Cert2Device, and the PI Gateway.

AV Service

Audio and Video player name and status.

HAM Info

Class, device, and software information.
