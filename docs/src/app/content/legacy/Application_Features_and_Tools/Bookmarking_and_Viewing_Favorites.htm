Bookmarking and Viewing Favorites

RIO allows you to bookmark entities or other pages that you frequently view for easy access. This section explains how to bookmark, view, and remove entities from your Favorites list.

Bookmarking an Entity

To add an item to your Favorites list:

Navigate to the page or entity you want to bookmark.

On the toolbar, click the Favorites icon .

From the drop-down list, select Add to favorites. RIO displays a confirmation.

Click Save as Favorite. If necessary, you can enter additional information in the text box before saving. RIO saves the page to your Favorites list on the Home page.

Once you have bookmarked a page, the Favorites icon changes to a solid star () to indicate it is a favorite.

Viewing Bookmarked Items

The entities you bookmark are listed in the Favorites panel on the Home page. Click a link in the panel to view the entity.

You can also access bookmarked favorites from the toolbar on any page in RIO. Click the Favorites icon and select an entry from the list.

 

Removing a Bookmarked Item

To remove an item from your Favorites list:

Navigate to the bookmarked page of the entity.

On the toolbar, click the Favorites icon.

Select Remove from favorites. RIO removes the item from your Favorites list.
