Accessing Recently Viewed Items

RIO allows you to easily access recently viewed items via the Recent History panel on the Home page and from the toolbar on any page.

On the toolbar, click the Recent Items icon. RIO displays the 10 most recent entities you have viewed.

Select the item you want to view from the list. RIO displays the entity's landing page.

To view more of your Recent History entries, click See all your history at the top of the item list. RIO displays your last 100 actions (including page views, navigated, quarantine etc.).  A sample is shown below.
