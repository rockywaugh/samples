Viewing Your Device Watches

RIO provides a Device Watch tab in the Cable Modem view as well as the Account and Division views. However, you can also view all the device watches you have configured across all devices.

To view all your Device Watches, select Device Watch from the User drop-down menu.

Click a hyperlinked Account Number or MAC Address to jump to the Account or Device Summary page.

To stop the watch, click Running in the Status column for the device, and then click Stop Watch.

To view additional details, click More+ in the Watch Results column.
